The compounding trick

Each cluster is an internal-link web: the broad hub page (e.g. "stages of building") links down to every specific page (roofing cost, ceiling cost, permits), and each specific page links back up. Google reads that structure as topical authority. You're not writing 25 articles — you're building one interlinked authority on "building in Ghana" that no single-post competitor can match.
