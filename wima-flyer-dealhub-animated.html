How to turn this into a video for WhatsApp & Facebook

  1. On a phone: open this page, tap Bigger until the flyer fills the width, then use your phone's screen recording (iPhone: Control Centre ⏺ · Android: Quick Settings → Screen record). Hit record, then tap Play / Replay, and let it run one full loop (about 6 seconds). Stop, and trim to the clean loop in your gallery.
  2. On a computer: use the built-in recorder (Mac: Shift-Cmd-5 · Windows: Win-G Game Bar) and record just the flyer area while it plays.
  3. Post it: the video goes straight to WhatsApp Status and the Facebook feed. The tall 9:16 shape is made for Status — no cropping.
Make it work harder: pair the post with a caption that captures interest — "Comment INTERESTED and we'll message you the day it opens." A teaser that builds a launch list beats one people just watch. And keep it spaced apart from your Dry Wall with Jamal launch posts, so the two don't compete for attention.
