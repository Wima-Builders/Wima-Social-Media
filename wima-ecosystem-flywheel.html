The Content Engine · Two Streams, One Audience

Social content and launch content are different jobs — and they feed each other

You were right that these aren't the same. One builds the audience; the other converts it. Kept distinct and looped together, each makes the other stronger.

Stream A · Brand & education

Builds the audience

  • Build-education, how-to, mistakes, land verification, diaspora guidance
  • Evergreen & SEO — compounds for years
  • Broad, top-of-funnel; TikTok, WhatsApp, the blog
  • Slow-burn trust. Sells nothing directly.
⇄
Stream B · Deal Hub & launch

Converts the audience

  • Product launches — Dry Wall with Jamal, then Kabinart, then Aquarium
  • Deal drops, campaigns, time-bound urgency
  • Bottom-of-funnel; transactional
  • Turns attention into orders.
How they enrich each other

Education content brings the crowd that launches convert. Launches throw off real stories, prices and project data that become the next round of education content — with proof no competitor can fake. The audience is shared; the jobs are separate. That loop is why neither stream should be watered down into the other.

Five Revenue Rails

One customer, one trust brand, five ways to earn

Each rail serves a different buyer situation with a different Wima role. Together they cover the entire build journey — and every rail is gated by the audience the content built.

Rail 01

Deal Hub — Aggregation

Principal · ops-heavy

Pool many buyers who need the same commodity so collective volume forces the price down. Works because materials are fungible and demand is simultaneous.

Buyer situation
Anyone needing cement, rods, blocks, roofing at a better price
How Wima earns
Materials margin on pooled volume
Leverage
The crowd — power comes from the pool
Discipline
Fulfilment weight; but it builds the supplier relationships that seed Rail 02
Rail 02

Tendering — Competition

Platform · asset-light

One buyer with a project big or specified enough that suppliers fight over it. Suppliers bid; the buyer picks. Wima holds no inventory — it owns the rails, the trust layer and the escrow.

Buyer situation
A full BOQ or bulk custom order worth competing for
How Wima earns
Platform take-rate / escrow — the true toll road
Leverage
Suppliers competing — power comes from the bid
Discipline
Trusted-bid, not cheapest-bid. Vetting + escrow prevent a race to the bottom and leakage
Rail 03

Live Commerce — Deal Drops

Phase 3–4 · ops-heavy

The QVC of building — but you sell the deal and the decision, not the raw commodity. Group-buy drops with a countdown, install demos that end in an order, "bring your BOQ" live sessions. Community pros host and vouch.

Buyer situation
An engaged follower ready to act on urgency + trust
How Wima earns
Volume on live-sold deals; pros sell on commission
Leverage
Entertainment + urgency + real-time objection handling
Discipline
82% of live sellers lose money — needs the audience AND mature fulfilment first
Rail 04

Vetted Execution — Extra Discount

Managed-build · you own the outcome

Get a further discount on Deal Hub or a tender if a Wima-vetted professional executes the project. The key is where the discount comes from — see the mechanic below. Wima crosses into turnkey-lite: oversight, guarantee, disputes.

Buyer situation
Wants materials and a trusted pro to build — one relationship
How Wima earns
Materials pull-through the pro brings + light "verified" membership — NOT a labour tax
Leverage
The audience brings jobs good pros can't get alone
Discipline
Real vetting + completion guarantee are mandatory — the pro's failure is your brand's failure
Rail 05

Bank Finance — Loans & Facilities

Referral · regulated

Partner with banks for construction and home-improvement loans. Wima refers pre-qualified borrowers — its own data has already profiled them — and earns origination commission. This is the accelerant that pulls demand through every other rail.

Buyer situation
Wants to build in 18 months, not 10 years of cash-flow stops
How Wima earns
Referral / origination commission per funded facility
Leverage
Behavioural data = high-quality leads banks pay more for
Discipline
Refer, don't lend. Responsible lending only — pushing bad debt burns the trust brand

The Finance Accelerant

Financing doesn't just add a rail — it speeds every other one

In a market where only 0.8% use a mortgage and building happens in slow cash-funded stages, a facility changes the physics: it collapses a decade-long build into an 18-month one — and every month saved is materials, tenders, execution and live deals pulled forward.

Without finance
The 10-year build

Buy land. Build to lintel when cash allows. Stop. Roof two years later. Materials bought in dribs at retail. Most builds stall half-done.

Wima plugs in a facility
The 18-month build

A construction or home-improvement facility funds the whole run. Materials bought in bulk through Deal Hub & tenders. A vetted pro executes on a schedule.

What Wima captures
Every rail, pulled forward

Origination commission from the bank — plus the materials, tender, execution and live-deal revenue the finance unlocked, years earlier.

The rate environment is turning in your favour

Ghana's policy rate has fallen sharply from its ~30% peak and cedi mortgage rates have eased toward the low 20s, with dedicated diaspora USD products from banks such as Republic (Seso Global), FNB, Stanbic and Absa in the ~10–12.5% range. As financing gets cheaper, more builds become viable — and Wima sits at the exact moment the borrower decides. (Recent market figures, 2026; verify live terms with each bank partner.)

The Vetted-Execution Discount · Where the money comes from

Make the discount real, or the trust brand pays for it

An "extra discount if our pro executes" only works if you know its source. Tax the pro's labour and the discount becomes an illusion the customer eventually spots. Fund it from materials volume and everyone's incentives align.

✕ The trap — labour tax

Wima takes a cut of the pro's fee. The pro quotes higher to cover it. The customer saves on materials but overpays on labour — funding your commission themselves. The "discount" is theatre, and the moment they do the math, the trust is gone.

✓ The fix — materials pull-through

The pro pulls all project materials through Deal Hub / Tendering. Wima earns on that volume, not on labour. The discount is real — funded by the extra materials the job brings. The pro gets steady work and simpler buying. No incentive to inflate labour.

The bonus this unlocks

The completion guarantee you must offer to stand behind a vetted pro is the same mechanism that stops both sides leaving the platform to transact off-book. One build solves quality liability and marketplace leakage at once. Layer a light "verified" membership on top for the badge — but keep volume, not a labour toll, as the engine.
