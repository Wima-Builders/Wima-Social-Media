The Content Library —
60 days, four ways to say each one.

The same daily message, written native for four channels: a WhatsApp version (convert), a Facebook version (community), a Video script (discovery), and a LinkedIn version (B2B — suppliers & banks). Month 1 launches Dry Wall with Jamal; Month 2 scales into Deal Hub and the diaspora. Everything above is a filter — tap Facebook to pull every Facebook post in one stream, tap a day for all four versions, or tap Pain-targeted to see which content answers which client fear.

How to use the filtersAll 4 platforms — pick a day, get every version of that day's message. WhatsApp / Facebook / Video / LinkedIn — pull every post for one channel in a single stream, each with a Copy button, so you can load a whole month into one platform in a sitting. By client pain — see which days answer which documented fear (hidden costs, betrayal from abroad, "who's on my site?"), then click through to that day. All 60 Days widens any view across both months.

How to run itAt your real pace: one shoot a week on a live job, 2–3 clips out, WhatsApp + Facebook steadily, LinkedIn when Gershon has something to say. Keep the 3:1 value-to-offer ratio. Each Sunday, rank by shares and WhatsApp messages started — not views. Swap [number], [area], and every price for your real figures before posting.
Don't end every post with "get a BOQ" — rotate the closeBOQ is the honesty proof, but if every post funnels to a quote, people file Wima under "the quote people" and scroll past. Keep the BOQ ask for the Offer days and the pure "how do I choose?" Educate days (~15 of 60). Everywhere else, close on curiosity, not a transaction — so the feed pulls people forward instead of selling on every beat:

• Jamal / People days → "Follow Jamal's builds" · "Ask Jamal anything" · a teaser for his next job.
• Entertain days → "Guess in the comments" · "Tag someone building" · "Answer tomorrow."
• Inspire / Proof days → "Wait till you see the next one 👀" · "Save this for your build" · "Follow for the reveal."
• Educate (general) days → "Which would you choose?" · "Steal this checklist" — teach, don't pitch.

Rule of thumb: value posts open a loop; only offer posts ask for the quote. The BOQ then lands harder because it's not on every post.
