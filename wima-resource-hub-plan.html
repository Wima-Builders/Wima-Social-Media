Document 20 · Plan · The Return-Traffic Engine

Give people a reason to come back — not just visit once.

A resource hub of checklists, calculators, guides and templates that people use again and again through their whole build. Each one is also a short video and a flyer. This document is the full library and the order to build it in — the tools come later.

Why this matters: content people use — a BOQ calculator, a checklist they tick off on site — brings them back far more than content they merely read. Every return visit is another chance to convert them into the community, and every resource is fuel for a week of social posts.

Part 1 · Why A Resource Hub

Four ways resources grow the business

01 · Return traffic

Tools pull people back

Nobody re-reads a blog post, but they return to use a cost estimator or tick off a site checklist — again and again, across a build that lasts years.

02 · Lead capture

Templates are lead magnets

"Download the free building checklist" in exchange for a WhatsApp number feeds the community list directly — the highest-value list you own.

03 · Content fuel

One resource, a week of posts

Every checklist item is a short video ("5 things to check before you pour your foundation"); every calculator is a demo clip and a flyer.

04 · Trust & SEO

Usefulness ranks and converts

Genuinely helpful tools rank on Google for build-intent searches and prove Wima is the trusted authority — before any sale.

Part 2 · The Stickiness Tiers

Not all resources pull equally — build in this order of impact

Tier 1 · Strongest return-driver

Interactive tools & calculators

BOQ calculator, cost-to-build estimator, materials quantity calculators, price tracker. People bookmark them, use them repeatedly, and share them on WhatsApp. This is the stickiness engine — build these first.

Tier 2 · Best lead magnets

Printable templates

Checklists, BOQ template, contractor agreement, milestone schedule. Gated behind a WhatsApp number, they convert visitors into community members.

Tier 3 · Endless content fuel

Checklists & guides

Stage-by-stage checklists and how-to guides. Lower return-pull on their own, but each one is a ready-made script for short videos and flyers — the raw material of the whole content engine.

The compounding effect

Every resource works three times: it pulls return visits, it captures the WhatsApp number, and it becomes a week of clips and flyers. Build the Tier-1 tools first, gate the templates for leads, and let the checklists feed the feed. The resource hub is the content engine and the community funnel in one.

The one line to hold

A shop gives people a reason to visit once. A toolbox gives them a reason to come back every week of their build — and by the time they're ready to buy, they're already ours.
