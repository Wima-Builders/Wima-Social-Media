Part 2 · Diaspora Pain Points → How Wima Serves Each

Tap a fear. See the content that speaks to it, and the service that resolves it.

Every diaspora pain is a content opportunity and a service Wima can render. This is how "serve them according to their pain points" becomes concrete — fear by fear.

The emotional core to lead with

Across the research, the deepest wound is betrayal by trusted people — "money sent for one thing, used for another," family and friends who mislead. Wima's single most powerful message: "You should never have to gamble on whether your own people will do right by you. We're the accountable party that isn't family and isn't a stranger with your cash."

Part 3 · How To Actually Reach The Diaspora

Where they are, and how to get in front of them

Meta geo-targeted ads (Facebook + Instagram)
The primary paid reach

Target Ghanaians in the UK, US, Canada by location + interest (Ghana, diaspora, property, "building in Ghana"). Location-based ads are the proven way to reach overseas buyers without leaving Accra. Click-to-WhatsApp as the action.

LinkedIn — the high-income diaspora
Under-used, growing 15.4%/yr

Reach diaspora professionals (doctors, engineers, finance) by their job — the premium USD-earning client. Founder-led thought leadership from Wima's strategy work builds the authority that converts them.

WhatsApp Business — the close
Where trust and deals live

Broadcast lists, a dedicated diaspora build-support group, real-time responses, encryption (matters to high-net-worth). Time-zone-aware replies. This is the relationship rail.

Diaspora Facebook groups & associations
Community reach

Ghanaian diaspora groups, hometown & church associations abroad. Show up with genuine value (the ethical-ambush comment strategy), not spam. These are dense pockets of exactly the right people.

Founder as the face
Become the trusted voice

The diaspora buys from a person they trust. Gershon fronting the education — on camera, in comments, in the group — outperforms a faceless brand. Be the influencer for your own niche.

The content that reaches AND converts them

Video is the trust-builder. Live site walk-throughs, drone footage, milestone video updates, and real project reveals directly answer the diaspora's core fear — lack of trustworthy visibility. Where competitors say "don't worry, it's a good place," Wima shows them, on video, on a schedule. That single behaviour beats every slogan.

Part 4 · The Fear-To-Trust Funnel

Move a diaspora builder from anxious stranger to loyal client

1 · Reach
Geo-targeted video + LinkedIn. A scam-story or "biggest diaspora mistake" video stops the scroll. They feel seen.
2 · Educate
Answer the fear. Land verification, milestone payments, "don't rely on family alone," how to monitor a build remotely. Trust compounds.
3 · Prove
Show, don't tell. Real project video updates, drone footage, a diaspora client's story. The Procurement Desk made visible.
4 · Relationship
Into WhatsApp. The diaspora support group + one-to-one, time-zone-aware. Questions answered by a real, accountable person.
5 · Serve
The Procurement Desk delivers. Milestone-linked payments, scheduled video updates, vetted execution. The fear is gone — they refer others.
