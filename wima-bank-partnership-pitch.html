Why Wima Borrowers Are Different

Not more leads — better ones, on the metrics your credit team cares about

01 · Reach

The unreachable market

We own the self-build and diaspora-build audience through trusted education content. Listing portals reach the tiny slice who buy finished homes; we reach the 90% who build — the exact users of your construction and home-improvement products.

02 · Conversion

Caught at the decision moment

Our borrowers aren't cold. They arrive having read build-cost guides, priced materials, and engaged vetted professionals — high intent, and warmed by a brand they already trust. A trusted introduction converts far above a cold marketing lead.

03 · Risk

Lower default, by design

We pre-screen against the criteria your underwriting uses — income band, project scope and budget, title status, geography and DTI fit — before we refer. With sector NPLs running high, better-qualified borrowers who default less is worth more to you than volume.

The argument that closes it

Your biggest cost isn't acquisition — it's default. Wima borrowers arrive with a real project, a real budget, and a real title, filtered before they reach you. We don't just lower your cost per loan; we raise the quality of your loan book.

Commercial Model

Performance-based — our incentives point the same way as yours

How you pay

  • Origination commission per funded facility — you pay only when a loan actually completes.
  • Optional small qualified-referral fee to cover screening, if preferred as a hybrid.
  • No retainer, no risk on unfunded leads. You carry cost only against booked business.

Why success-based, deliberately

  • It aligns us with loan quality, not lead quantity — we're paid to send you borrowers who fund and perform.
  • It protects our brand: we never profit from pushing a facility a borrower shouldn't take.
  • It makes the pilot easy to say yes to — you risk nothing until a loan books.

The Proof · A 90-Day Pilot

Don't take the claim — measure it

We won't ask you to believe our conversion or risk numbers. We'll prove them against your own baseline, in one quarter, with nothing at stake until a facility funds.

  1. Define the funnel. Agree the qualifying criteria and the referral hand-off with your construction/mortgage desk.
  2. Deliver a target volume of pre-qualified referrals over 90 days, each screened on income, project, title, geography and DTI fit.
  3. Measure against your baseline — application-to-funding conversion and early-arrears rate, Wima referrals vs your current channel.
  4. Set the rate from real data. The per-funded-facility commission is agreed from the pilot's proven conversion and quality, not a guess.

Specific volume and conversion targets to be set jointly at kick-off, so both sides commit to numbers we can hold each other to.

Roles & Compliance

We refer. You lend. The borrower is protected.

Wima is an introducer, not a lender or a licensed financial adviser. Every affordability assessment, credit decision and disbursement remains entirely with the bank. We screen for basic fit and obtain the borrower's consent to be referred — nothing more.

We only refer where a facility genuinely serves the borrower. Responsible lending isn't a compliance box for us — it's how we protect the trust that makes our referrals valuable to you. Introducer arrangements to be structured for full Bank of Ghana compliance before launch.

Why Now

Rates are falling into the range where the mass market becomes bankable

As the Reference Rate settles and diaspora products hold near 11.5%, more of the self-build market crosses the affordability line for the first time. Whoever owns the trusted relationship with the builder at that moment owns the referral. Wima already does.

The one line

We sit with the borrower at the exact moment they decide to build — and we can hand you a qualified, lower-risk facility before they ever think to walk into a branch. Let's prove it in 90 days.
